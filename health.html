Physical Wellness

Physical activity and a healthy, plant-based diet are key for heart disease and breast cancer prevention.

Regular practice helps women maintain better physical and mental health, energy levels, and self-esteem. It can also help with several medical conditions.

Mental Wellness

Mental wellness encompasses a balance of spiritual and mental health. It’s about being aware of your emotions and knowing how to deal with stress and life’s changes.

Life experiences can influence their vulnerability to emotional disturbances, and can affect decision making, and the ability to live life functionally.

Hygiene

Regular bathing is essential for everyone. The habit of skipping baths can lead to various skin infections such as dry skin and fungal infections.

Routine health checkup with your gynecologist is necessary to maintain health.
